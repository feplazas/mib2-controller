Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to MIB2 Controller ("we," "our," or "the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. MIB2 Controller is a specialized tool designed for controlling and configuring MIB2 STD2 Technisat Preh infotainment units via USB connection.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices. By using MIB2 Controller, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

MIB2 Controller operates primarily as a local application and does not require user registration or personal accounts. However, we may collect the following information:

  • Configuration Data: Settings and preferences you configure within the app (stored locally on your device)
  • Vehicle Information: VIN and VCRN numbers you optionally provide for FEC code generation (processed locally, not transmitted)
  • Custom FEC Codes: Feature Enable Codes you create or import (stored locally)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we may automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Android version, device model, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features accessed, diagnostic logs, and error reports (stored locally)
  • USB Device Data: Information about connected USB devices (vendor ID, product ID, device name) for compatibility purposes

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

Important: MIB2 Controller does NOT collect, transmit, or store:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Payment or financial information
  • Contacts, photos, or media files
  • Browsing history or web activity

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Provide Core Functionality: Enable USB communication with MIB2 units, execute spoofing operations, and generate FEC codes
  • Improve User Experience: Remember your preferences and settings across app sessions
  • Diagnose Issues: Analyze diagnostic logs (stored locally) to troubleshoot connection or compatibility problems
  • Ensure Compatibility: Detect and support various USB adapter chipsets

All data processing occurs locally on your device. We do not transmit your data to external servers or third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All app data, including settings, diagnostic logs, and FEC codes, is stored locally on your Android device using secure storage mechanisms (AsyncStorage). This data remains on your device and is not synchronized to cloud services.

4.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your data:

  • Data encryption for sensitive configuration files
  • Secure USB communication protocols
  • No transmission of data over the internet
  • Regular security audits and updates

4.3 Data Retention

Your data is retained locally on your device until you manually delete it or uninstall the app. You can clear diagnostic logs and reset settings at any time through the app's Settings screen.

5. Permissions Explained

MIB2 Controller requires the following Android permissions to function:

Permission Purpose
USB Access Required to communicate with USB-Ethernet adapters and MIB2 units
Internet Access Used only for accessing online FEC code generators (optional feature)
Notifications Display alerts for USB connection status and operation results

You can review and revoke these permissions at any time through your device's Settings → Apps → MIB2 Controller → Permissions.

6. Third-Party Services

6.1 External FEC Code Generators

MIB2 Controller includes an optional feature to access external FEC code generation websites (e.g., vwcoding.ru). When you use this feature:

  • You are directed to a third-party website in an in-app browser
  • That website's privacy policy applies to any data you provide
  • We do not control or have access to data you submit to these sites
  • We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these external services

6.2 No Analytics or Tracking

MIB2 Controller does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not collect usage statistics, crash reports, or behavioral data for external analysis.

7. Children's Privacy

MIB2 Controller is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to delete such information.

8. International Users

MIB2 Controller is designed for global use and supports multiple languages (Spanish, English, German). Since all data is stored locally on your device, no cross-border data transfers occur. However, if you access external FEC code generation websites, those services may be subject to different jurisdictional laws.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. When we make changes:

  • The "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy will be revised
  • Significant changes will be communicated through an in-app notification
  • Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

10. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View all data stored by the app through the Settings screen
  • Deletion: Clear diagnostic logs, reset settings, or uninstall the app to remove all data
  • Export: Export diagnostic logs and FEC codes for backup purposes
  • Opt-Out: Disable optional features (e.g., online FEC code generation) at any time

Since all data is stored locally, you have full control over your information at all times.
