Misuse Prevention Through
Hardware and Safety Features
How Hardware Requirements Prevent Misuse
1.
Specialized Equipment Barrier
MIB2 units cost $200–500 and need 12V power plus specific adapters, limiting casual access.
2.
Technical Knowledge Requirement
Users need knowledge of automotive networking, FEC code workflows, and command-line operations.
3.
Limited Scope of Functionality
App only communicates with MIB2 units (IP: 192.168.1.1) and cannot access unrelated vehicle systems.
Built-in Safety Features
Automatic Backup
App prompts a local timestamped backup before FEC changes.
Confirmation Dialogs
Warnings and explicit confirmations for risky operations.
Read-Only Mode
Diagnostics and device info viewable without modification rights.
Audit Trail
All operations logged with timestamps; accessible in Settings → View Logs.
Why This Matters
The combination of hardware requirements, knowledge barriers, and safety features helps ensure responsible use by technicians, owners, and researchers rather than casual users.
