Legal Compliance Under DMCA Exemptions

Vehicle Diagnosis and Repair: Computer programs in motorized land vehicles when circumvention is necessary for diagnosis, repair, or lawful modification by the authorized owner.

Interoperability: Computer programs where circumvention is undertaken on a lawfully acquired device for enabling interoperability with other programs.
37 C.F.R. § 201.40(b)(12)
Security Research: Computer programs where circumvention is undertaken on a lawfully acquired device for good-faith security research purposes.
